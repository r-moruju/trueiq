🧠 What Your IQ Score Really Says About You

What does your IQ score mean? Whether above average or not, it tells you more than just a number. Let’s explore what it really says about your mind.

🧪 What Is an IQ Test?

IQ stands for Intelligence Quotient. It’s a measure of logic, pattern recognition, and abstract thinking—not your knowledge or creativity.

📊 IQ Score Ranges

IQ Range Category What It Suggests
130+ Very Superior Highly gifted; qualifies for high IQ societies
120–129 Superior Above average reasoning and logic
110–119 High Average Strong problem-solving ability
90–109 Average Most people fall into this category
80–89 Low Average May struggle with abstract or timed tasks
Below 80 Below Average Might indicate learning difficulties
